SOLUTE is a technology company that delivers innovative products using state-of-the-art software, hardware, network technologies and DevOps automation. Our products span the fields of cyber security, network virtualization, global positioning, navigation, aviation electronics, UAV/UAS command systems, knowledge management and collaboration systems. Our depth of engineering support, technical expertise, architecture design and enterprise solutions provide a uniquely trusted and influential skillset to our customers and industry giants alike.  We are a company of innovative professionals thriving in a highly motivating work environment that fosters creativity and independent thinking. If you are a motivated individual with a desire to create exceptional products and support our service men and women, now is a great time to join SOLUTE!  SOLUTE is looking for a Systems Engineer to provide technical guidance and direction to some of SOLUTEs most critical systems. This Systems Engineer will primarily be responsible for software and systems engineering related to a variety of internal projects as well as providing technical leadership and software architecture decisions for programs. Good candidates have strong ethics, dependability and ownership. Candidates should also have a desire to provide hands-on coding as needed and have great organization and process management.
