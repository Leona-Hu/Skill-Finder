Java Software Engineer w/ Java8 & LambdasLocation: New York, NY or San Francisco, CAHave the opportunity to join a dynamic engineering team located in New York City or San Francisco, who is recognized in the Open Source Community to the most innovative best in breed Low-Latency Analytics platform leveraging Spark.  This builds Spark frameworks and toolkits.  The group aims to create standardized solutions to complex yet common problems across R&D and offer as a service.
