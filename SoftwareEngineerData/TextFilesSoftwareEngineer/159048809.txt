Software Engineer MassiveU is an award-winning education Platform-As-A-Service company based in Naples, FL. We serve the world's leading education companies by converting their curricula into project based, social learning experiences. As an organization focused on the delivery of digital learning experiences and education technology solutions that meet market and client needs, we're looking for an A-Player Software Engineer who has a passion for creating transformational technologies and systems, and is interested in professional development, challenge and growth.Reporting to the CIO of the company, the Software Engineer is part of the development team and participates in the development and maintenance of the company’s primary technology offerings, including new product development.
