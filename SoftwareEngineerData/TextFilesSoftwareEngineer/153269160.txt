TITLE: Software EngineerDIVISION: Kyocera Documents Solutions Development America, Inc. Los Angeles DEPARTMENT: Software EngineeringLOCATION: Gardena, CaliforniaSTATUS: ExemptPOSITION REPORTS TO: Section Manager, PDL SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITY:We are looking for an exceptional software engineer with experience in designing, implementing and maintaining core technology development or product projects. This engineer will be working on a fast-paced team with direct interaction with the hardware team. Responsibilities will include the design, development, integration, and validation of complex software for our next generation of products. This position will be responsible for the development of products in accordance with our software development process; execute system software engineering at the highest level; contribute in designing, coding, maintaining and debugging software components in complex systems and work on projects individually and collaboratively with other engineers. MAJOR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: QUALIFICATIONS:An undergraduate degree is required; an advanced degree is desirable.CONTACTS:Internal contacts within Software Development, Project Management Office and Testing department.AUTHORITY:The incumbent is authorized to take any reasonable action necessary to carry out the assigned responsibilities; provided that such action is consistent with sound management andcost-effective practices and is consistent with product planning guidelines and department objectives. Work under Manager Software Development or Project Manager. A certain degree of creativity and latitude is required. MISCELLANEOUS: Special projects as assigned by the Manager Software Development.Essential Physical Functions:The physical demands described are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job, with or without reasonable accommodation:· Occasional lifting of computer, monitor, or small printer not heavier than 40 pounds.· Typing, writing, reading· Seeing, hearing and speaking, both nearby and at a distance· Frequent sitting· Standing, walking, kneeling.Essential Mental Functions:Analytical skills, troubleshooting skills, verbal and written communications in English.This is a general description of the duties and responsibilities most frequently required of this position. The company may from time to time request that the incumbent perform other related tasks and assume reasonable responsibilities that have not been specifically included in this description.
