BigBear, Inc. currently has an immediate position for a talented and passionate JAVA Software Engineer in the Charlottesville, VA area to build high-performing, scalable, enterprise-grade applications. You will be responsible for server-side application development while providing expertise in the full software development lifecycle, from concept and design to testing. You will collaborate directly with customers to ensure our products meet and exceed operational requirements.
