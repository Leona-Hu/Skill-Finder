Sr. Backup Systems EngineerSr. Backup Engineer Summary: The Storage & Backup Engineer is responsible for daily Backup and SAN administration across a range of technologies including Pure Storage, NetApp, Commvault, Data Domain, Cohesity, EXAGRID, Netbackup, Veeam, StorageTek, and various other tape and disk targets. This position requires a detailed understanding of a wide range of enterprise SAN and Backup technologies, operating systems, applications, and business objectives. This individual participates in technical research, design, development, troubleshooting and support to innovate within the infrastructure.SAN & Backup Essential Duties and Responsibilities:Support the installation, configuration, and maintenance of various backup technology solutions. Experience with disk and tape backup targets. Able to support a wide range of technologies. Provides a deep technical subject matter expertise and leadership for analyzing hardware and software needs. Work independently and with third party vendors to design, install, support, and upgrade various applications to support business needs. Monitor the environment for performance and stability issues to meet Service Level Objectives. Maintain current system patching levels. Provides oversight and tracking of support tickets, ensuring that issues and requests are resolved in a timely manner. Pro-actively manage the environment and correct potential or present issues as identified. Consult with infrastructure operations, development personnel, and support teams to provide technical advice and to resolve problems. Provides senior technical consulting internally to teammates and externally to a variety of IT teams. Follow internal procedures for change management, incident management, and escalation. Work with peers across multiple business units. Participate in a 24/7 on-call rotation. Other responsibilities will be assigned as required.
