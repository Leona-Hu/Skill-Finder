Senior Software Engineer - Data Technology Apps (Java, Python or JavaScript)Location: Princeton, NJHave the opportunity to join a fast growing Computer Software firm located in Princeton, NJ that runs on Data. You will work as a Senior Software Engineer or Mid-to-Intermediate level Software Engineer within the Data Technologies group at this firm working on the design and development of high quality, distributed and scalable platforms or applications. Your knowledge of all phases of the SDLC will enable you to partner with business teams on requirements gathering, leading project priorities and final product. You will have the opportunity to work on multiple projects in a fast-paced environment, take ownership of solutions and strive for project and company success. You’ll be responsible for the systems that onboard all the referential data that drive the firm's applications and enterprise systems. As this firm's clients are shifting more and more to rely on machines to interpret data and drive insights, this team is utilizing cutting edge technologies to deliver unparalleled data quality. By joining Data Technologies, you will help us improve the accuracy, coverage, timeliness, and accessibility of the firm's data to service the firm's clients across all of the firm's products. You'll need to have (Required Skills):• BA, BS, MS, PhD in Computer Science, Engineering or related technology field• A strong understanding of data structures, algorithms, multi-threading and design patterns• Strong object-oriented analysis, design and coding skills• Object Oriented Programming experience using any of the following Java, Python or JavaScript (client/server-side)• 3+ years of experience with developing Distributed Systems in a UNIX/Linux environment• Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills• Excellent communication and interpersonal skillsWe'd love to see (Any of these skills are considered a plus):• A Master's degree in Computer Science or equivalent field• Familiarity with search technologies such as SOLR and Elastic Search• Experience with or exposure to the Hadoop Ecosystem including HDFS, MapReduce, HBase, Hive and Pig• Familiarity with NoSQL databases such as Couchbase and MongoDB• An Understanding of relational database including SQL
