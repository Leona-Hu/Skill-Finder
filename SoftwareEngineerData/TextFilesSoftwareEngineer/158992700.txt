As such, we hold you to the same high standards as anybody else in the company.Just like our gameplay experiences, Blizzard internships are a fully immersive experience. We want interns who are enthusiastic about what they want to do for a living and excited to come in every day to learn and contribute in this hyper-creative atmosphere. Your hobbies and interests can also play in your favor.
