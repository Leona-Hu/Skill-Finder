Does Blizzard sound like an awesome place to work? Do you want to make it even more awesome?Blizzard Entertainment is seeking enthusiastic Associate Software Engineers to join our Corporate Applications group in Irvine, CA. The Corporate Applications team must be responsive to changing and emergent business needs. They are inquisitive in technical discussions, embrace production processes, and aren’t afraid to fail.You will need a strong sense of ownership and commitment to continuous improvement.
