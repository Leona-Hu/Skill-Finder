Systems Engineer III (Senior)Clearance: SecretLocation: Arlington, VAType: Exempt, Full Time, RegularDescription: The Systems Engineer III (Senior) will have extensive experience in (1) executing the full complement of systems engineering activities from requirements mapping, to architecture, design, implementation, and test, through deployment and operations and support of both commercial and government software applications; (2) implementation of the Department of Defense (DoD) acquisition process through the appropriate application of laws, policies, directives, and guidance; and (3) preparing recommendations with sufficient rationale to advise senior leader decisions that enable program success and customer acceptance of the application/solution. The individual must (a) understand requirements definition and traceability mapping into the proposed commercial solution/application; (b) be able to develop acquisition documentation; and (c) have the knowledge and skills to define key processes supporting all engineering aspects of program management, requirements and data mapping, and baseline management operations, to include assessing and overseeing technical capabilities, data, system design, development, implementation, and test of system solutions.Essential Duties and Responsibilities: (Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position)· Lead / conduct requirements traceability and analysis, design, development, verification and validation, deployment, change management, and transition activities by applying a broad and deep set of engineering expertise of government and commercial systems· Ensure compliance with DoD and Army architecture, data, and acquisition policies addressed in DoD 5000 and 8000 series· Contribute to systems engineering aspects of contract procurement planning, solicitation, award, data, and performance monitoring· Align engineering activities with program acquisition strategies to include ensuring schedule is achievable and adequate technical performance measures/controls are in place· Define / review business process models, architectures, designs, technical specifications, and implementation strategies (e.g., engineering, security, testing, training, deployment)· Develop / provide key content for acquisition documentation to achieve milestone decisions (e.g., Systems Engineering Plan (SEP), Test Evaluation Master Plan (TEMP), Information Support Plan (ISP), Program Protection Plan (PPP))· Lead key processes, to include Requirements Management, Data Mapping, Release Management, Configuration Management, and Risk Management· Manage, develop, assess, and process engineering change proposals and requests, to include performing technical analyses/ feasibility studies and identifying system impacts associated with new requirements, and requirements changes· Develop release plans aligned to capability priorities· Lead Systems Engineering Working Integrated Product Teams (WIPTs)· Communicate project information through presentations, technical reports, and / or white papersNon-Essential Duties:Other duties as assignedSupervisory Responsibilities: This position does not have formal supervisory responsibilities; however, it will require management of projects and deliverables that may be supported by program staff (Government and / or Contractor).Minimum Qualifications: (To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential
