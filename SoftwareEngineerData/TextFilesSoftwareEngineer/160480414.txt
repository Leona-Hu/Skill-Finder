Job Description:Bastion Technologies Inc. is looking for a NASA Software Safety Engineer to support the Safety & Mission Assurance (SMA) Office at the Marshall Space Flight Center in Huntsville, Alabama. The Software Safety Engineer will be responsible for the following activities in support of programs across the MSFC and primarily the Space Launch System (SLS) program: Software Assurance (SA) analyses and assessments related to quality, reliability, and safety; review and analysis of engineering design concepts and software assurance plans; assessments of FMEA, Hazard Analysis, and FTA products; development and implementation of safety engineering approaches; and assuring compliance with NASA safety polices and standards for hardware and software. The successful candidate will use his or her strong embedded software background, software safety expertise, management experience and NASA domain experience to evaluate target programs to ensure safety and mission success, to work with a growing team of engineers performing software assurance tasks, to plan and manage those tasks, and work closely with the customer. Occasional travel may be required. Work is performed at the NASA MSFC in Huntsville, Alabama and will require frequent interaction with multiple external and internal customers.
