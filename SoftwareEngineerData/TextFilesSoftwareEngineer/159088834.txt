Position Description:Works under the direct supervision of others to perform information systems engineering, functional analysis of ATC/M system requirements, development, production, or test and evaluation of related information systems. Includes, but not limited to, technical alternative analyses, acquisition management of information systems, and information management tools. Duties include, but are not limited to, performing hardware and software analyses to provide comparative data or performance characteristics and suitability within the existing environment, usually under the guidance of a more senior leader or manager, Provides recommendations on configuration management issues. Assists in the design and development of operational use cases and their integration as part of the program office requirements management, change request management, and overall configuration management functions, including management of the configuration controlled baselines, Information Assurance and EA.Education:0-5 years experience. A BA/BS in a technical field, to include but not limited to: engineering, math, physics, chemistry, biology, meteorology/earth sciences, aeronautical science, computer science, decision science, instructional system design, management/computer information systems, aviation/information technology, information assurance, aeronautics, human factors/psychology, air traffic control/management, etc. is required. A Masters degree in any field (e.g MS, MA, MBA) counts for three years experience, and a PhD counts for three years experience. An additional five years experience may be substituted for the degree stated.
