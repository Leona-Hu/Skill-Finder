Aerospace and defense company seeks Full-Time Systems Engineers! Positions provide critical systems engineering support to military C4ISR technology program offices for the U.S. Air Force at Hanscom Air Force Base. Responsibilities include: conducting reviews on program portfolio to evaluate and recommend plans, improving systems engineering programs/processes, managing program technical baselines, and designing and performing system level tests to assure that subsystems and system performance is achieved in accordance with approved specifications. This role also prepares technical documents which define, specify, and recommend system/subsystem characteristics and delivers engineering reports and technical presentations for planning, implementation and corrective action using application of engineering principles. The position provides system/program engineering, technical support and technical training to support programs including but not limited to: acquisition, development, integration, sustainment, continued evolution, deployment/fielding, and operations oversight.Qualified candidates will have relevant systems engineering support experience. Work experience on Department of Defense systems engineering projects highly preferred. Applicants should be fluent in MS Office, be extremely professional and self-motivated, and have excellent communication and organizational skills. Knowledge of the principles, policies and practices of systems acquisition and engineering as well as knowledge of roles and relationships of Department of Defense and the Air Force are highly desired. Ability to qualify for a U.S. Government Security Clearance is an essential requirement for performance of this position. Highly competitive salary and full company benefits. Apply now!
