Job SummaryWe are currently searching for a Staff Software Engineer to join our Avionics Design Assurance Center (DAC) team in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.We're looking for an experienced engineering professional who is interested in working together with others to achieve excellence in design and efficient lifecycle execution. This position is responsible for working closely with software engineering teams to provide quality-focused technical and process oversight of product developments throughout their lifecycle.As a DAC team member supporting Avionics Engineering, you will help ensure designs meet quality expectations, as well as customer, regulatory, and other requirements. The role requires the ability to influence the adoption of engineering best practices to identify and eliminate defects before delivery.This position requires the applicant to have demonstrated experience in software development process and methods, including planning, requirements capture, design, implementation and integration, verification, and validation.Success in this position requires a Passion for Quality, Process and Continuous Improvement and the ability to build rapport with the Engineering project teams in order to influence the teams do the "right thing for the right reason".Job Responsibilities:LEAD DESIGN ASSURANCE ENGAGEMENT. Serve as the primary DAC point of contact for assigned engineering projects. Develop project-specific quality plans for new product developments as required. Collaborate with other DAC Subject Matter Experts on your team to ensure appropriate technical engagement with projects. Represent the DAC at customer events.ASSESS ENGINEERING EFFECTIVENESS. Ensure engineering efforts are performed effectively and efficiently, meeting customer, company, and applicable regulatory requirements and quality expectations. Contribute to requirement and design reviews, test readiness reviews, and other project reviews and team meetings.MENTOR TEAMS. Mentor engineering teams as needed throughout the development lifecycle in the areas of process, tools, design quality, and regulatory requirements to ensure robust and problem-free delivery.EVALUATE PROJECTS. Perform proactive, ongoing assessments to determine the health of projects using objective measurements and audits. Produce metrics reports for management review. Employ methods such as root cause analysis as appropriate. Analyze project data for trends and systemic issues. Recommend and facilitate corrective actions. FACILITATE CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT. Lead the efforts of cross-functional teams to ensure that quality issues seen in legacy products are avoided in new designs. Assist with engineering project planning activities, ensuring plans account for lessons learned across the enterprise.Basic Qualifications:This position requires a Bachelor's degree in the appropriate discipline and 7 years of relevant experience or an Advanced degree in the appropriate discipline and 5 years of relevant experience.  In the absence of a degree, 11 years of relevant experience is required.U.S. citizenship or granted U.S. permanent residence ("Green Card" holder)Experience creating and following a documented development process with working knowledge and demonstrated applicationKnowledge of effective Design Quality Assurance principlesPreferred Qualifications:DOORS proficientJIRA proficientExperience in Agile development environment, e.g., SAFeProject experience with industry and regulatory standards such as DO-178B/C, SAE ARP4754A, and/or AS9100JAMA experience
