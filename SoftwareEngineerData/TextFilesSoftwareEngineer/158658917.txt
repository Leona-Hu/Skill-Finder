Senior Systems Engineer - 19194Position Location Virginia Beach, VARequisition # 19194# of Openings 2HII  Mission Driven Innovative Solutions Inc. (HII-MDIS) provides mission-critical engineering and technical service to Information Technology, Federal Civilian, and National Security, Aerospace & Defense and International government and commercial customers at over 100 locations worldwide.HII - MDIS is currently seeking a Senior Systems Engineer to work out of their Virginia Beach, VA office.Duties and Responsibilities (to include but not be limited to the following):Develop Fit-for-Purpose Architectural Descriptions through data collection and the use of models and viewpoints.Develop Equipment and System Level Diagnostics for unique hardware components within the target Combat System Suite.Develop Common Display products and practices for use across the Navys Combat System Enterprise.Analyze system software requirements to identify the hardware requirements for the proper operation of the system software in its target operational environment.Recommend hardware solutions to meet systems software requirements and support future upgrades.Job performance requires adequate visual acuity and manual dexterity for meeting the requirements of the job. An office work environment is normally encountered.May occasionally require working in an industrial or shipboard environment.Qualifications and Experience:Required Minimum of ten (10) years of full-time professional experience in Systems Engineering.Three (3) of these years must have been full-time experience at the system level in any of the following areas: Senior level Engineering requirements definition, system design parameters, concepts operation, risk assessment, producibility studies / analyses, test and evaluation, post-delivery support; Systems Engineering of complex engineering project requiring task effort monitoring through Cost Schedule and Deliverable status determination and skilled in Risk Mitigation practices.Knowledge of U.S. Navy surface ship combat systems and embedded training systems such as AN/USQ-T46 (V) BFTT System, AN/USQ-T48 (V) Trainer, AN/USQ-T47 BFTT Electronic Warfare Trainer (BEWT), Simulator/Stimulator Systems (TSSS), Damage Control Training Management System (DCTMS), Navigation Seamanship and Ship Handling Trainer (NSST) on various classes of ships.Experience with managing C4ISR shipboard projects of varying complexity.Experience in scheduling and identifying activities in accordance with 9090.310 as required for system upgrades/modifications, and new system installations.Knowledge of ISO 9000, Navy Quality Management and Navy Standard Items.10 years relevant (DoD/USN) experience with Bachelor's degree; OR 8 years relevant experience with Master's degree; OR an additional 4 years of specific job experience with a HS diploma may be substituted for the Bachelor's degree requirement for this job. This experience is in addition to the relevant years of experience listed with the job's experience requirements.Travel up to 25% of the time.Preferred Previous experience interfacing with the NSWC and NAVSEA customers.Familiarity with Surface Force Readiness Manual (SFRM), Surface Force Exercise Manual (SFEM) and use of the Master Scheduling Program (MSP).Familiarity with both shore and shipboard training exercises and FST distributed events.Multiple tours on naval ships.Familiarity with the Optimized Fleet Response Plan (OFRP), Fleet Response Training Plan (FRTP), and fleet training policy documents, including the Fleet Training Continuum (FTC).Recent experience in FST events, naval exercises, and exercise/event planning.Security:All candidates will be subject to a pre-employment background investigation and drug screening per HII requirementsThe selected applicants will be subject to a security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.U.S. citizen with current DoD Secret Clearance is required.Huntington Ingalls Industries is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, veteran status, or any other protected class.
