Casne Engineering is seeking mid-level engineers and technologists to join our professional services team for global data center operations and expand our East coast presence. This team is responsible for analyzing customer operational and business needs and helping to design, install, test, and support best fit solutions for data center infrastructure management (DCIM), energy management, condition-based maintenance (CBM), and real-time monitoring of critical systems. Primary responsibilities include working within a cross functional team to apply best practices approaches to solving common challenges in data centers and other critical facilities. Candidates must have hands-on experience configuring and integrating instrumentation, software, and devices common to EPMS, BMS, BCM, and CMMS applications.
