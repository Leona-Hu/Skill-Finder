SummaryAt Sorenson Communications we are passionate about delivering the world’s most innovative and high quality communication services and products for the deaf and hard-of-hearing communities, and we are looking for a Software Engineer in Test II that can match that passion. This person will work alongside equally committed members of a brilliant and friendly team to develop and test high quality Server and Videophone products that help our customers communicate using American Sign Language. Under the direction of a QA Manager, the Sr. Software Engineer II in Test will improve the quality of our products by reviewing test plans, writing and executing Automated test cases, documenting defects as they are found and repaired, and reporting daily on the progress they have made to make our products even better Essential Duties and Responsibilities· Test all aspects of Sorenson Communications Server software products.· Work as part of a product development team.· Plan testing efforts and create test timelines and schedules.· Define test cases and prepare test plans.· Design and implement test automation scripts/programs.· Mentor test technicians on the team and assign them duties as needed.· Monitor, measure, and improve software development and test processes.· Diagnose product problems and assist Technical Support as needed.· Other duties as assigned. Knowledge/Skills/Abilities Required Experience/Education/Certifications· Experience testing client/server based software and distributed systems. Other General Requirements/Additional Notes· Good written communication skills are required.· Able to manage small projects assigned to them and have the ability to delegate as necessary.· Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, power tools, and to handle other computer components.· Ability to sit at workstation for long periods of time completing testing, documentation, or other assignments· Lifting and transporting of moderately heavy objects, such as computers and peripherals.· Work environment – office cubicles.· Must be able to lift a minimum of 50 pounds.· Positive attitude, team player, good interpersonal communication skills and able to work across company departments.· This position has access to highly confidential, sensitive information relating to the employees, customers, and technologies of Sorenson Communications. It is essential that applicant possess the requisite integrity to maintain the information in strictest confidence. Equal Employment Opportunity:Sorenson Communications is an Equal Opportunity, Affirmative Action Employer.
