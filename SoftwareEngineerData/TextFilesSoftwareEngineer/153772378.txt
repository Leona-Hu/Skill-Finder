Software Engineer - Trading Systems (Mid.-to-Intermediate levels)Location: New York, NYHave the opportunity to join a Financial Services software product firm located in New York City working as a Mid.-level, Intermediate-level or Senior Software Engineer working within the Trading Systems software engineering team. Trading Systems empowers the biggest players in the financial world to manage their portfolios, execute and settle their trades, and make decisions that move the markets. Trillions of dollars pass through this firm's trading systems on a daily basis so their applications need to be fast, accurate and scalable. Their integrated platforms interact with a number of agencies and governments around the world, and they use the best technology the industry offers to build them.This firm is looking to hire full stack engineers who specialize in everything from the UI / UX to backend server-side development and the team is always looking for new ways to make their products better. As engineers, this team lives at the intersection of technology and finance, so you'll have the opportunity to learn about the financial markets and suggest new ways this group can lead the industry.As a Software Engineer in Trading Systems, you'll be able to lead projects, partner with the business and meet clients to understand their workflows. This team works primarily in C++, but they believe a smart engineer can learn any language with some ramp up time, so they are willing to consider anyone with Object Oriented programming skills and willingness to work with C++. If you've got a strong grasp of Computer Science fundamentals, this team will want to speak to you. This firm has several has opportunities in all phases of the SDLC, so if you want to make an immediate impact and work on the financial world's toughest technical challenges, you should apply today.You need to have (Required Skills):• Good knowledge of Computer Science fundamentals (Algorithms, Data Structures, Object Oriented Programming, etc.)• 2-5+ years of experience with object-oriented programming such as Java, C#, C++, or Python with a willingness to work in C++.• Strong problem solving skillsAny of the following skills would be a plus (Desired Skills):• Experience with multithreading• Familiarity with SQL, Python and JavaScript• Experience with high volume, high availability distributed systems• Interest in the financial markets
