What does Aras Development do?Leading company in the PLM Software Space- Very well funded with over 300 customers worldwideAras Development is responsible for developing, testing, supporting and maintaining a suite of applications based on Aras Innovator development framework.We are a process oriented team with a focus on core engineering principles. Our team of Software Solutions Engineers is focused on identifying and solving problems that can be found when working in an open development framework like Aras Innovator.What is a Software Solutions Engineer?At the core, a Software Solutions Engineer is a Software Developer. However, a solutions engineer is the Jill/Jack of all trades in our team.·You will participate in collecting and defining requirements·You will participate in developing and testing the product·You will participate in customization of the product for customers·You will help to establish and document procedures for new software featuresSoftware Solutions Engineer is one of the most challenging jobs in our organization. The main required quality of the candidate is her/his versatility. A solution engineer must be prepared to develop a wide range of skills and apply them to a variety of challenges in a single week.What skills does a Software Solutions Engineer need?We are looking for engineers with strong problem solving skills, and a grounding in engineering principles.•Experience with C# and JavaScript is a plus•Experience with Web Application development is a plus•Experience with SQL is a plusWhile you may not have all required skills when you start, you will learn and apply them in the job.
