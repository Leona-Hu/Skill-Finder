Flexera Support Engineering is a critical member of the Flexera customer and employee experience. Our goal is to get the right information, to the right person at the right time. As the Support Engineering Data Scientist, you will play a critical and leadership role in shaping our ability to deliver on that goal. The ideal candidate will independently run analytical data models to evaluate Support process and delivery across a broad range of daily Support Engineering activities. The Support Engineer Data Scientist will be responsible for providing improvement opportunities in case deflection and time to resolution by developing analytical solutions to support problems using a variety of mathematical modeling and programming techniques.
