 The Advertising Research Foundation is seeking a data scientist with one to three years of experience to conduct primary research in the fields of marketing and communications, ad tech, neuroscience of creative testing and advertising impact, attribution and return on investment, and cross-platform/cross-device media research. These positions offer access to extremely rich data sources, exposure to very senior industry leaders and an opportunity to work on front-burner issues and problems in the media/advertising ecosystem. The ARF serves a diverse membership of 400 companies drawn from across the advertising & media landscape (for details: www.thearf.org). Candidates should have at least an MS in statistics, physics, operations research, applied mathematics or related scientific field. Applicants should have strong skills in machine learning and proficiency in Matlab, R or Python as well as strong written and verbal communication skills and the ability to translate complex analysis into simple terms, strong critical thinking skills and follow through. The positions are located in our Midtown Manhattan offices.
