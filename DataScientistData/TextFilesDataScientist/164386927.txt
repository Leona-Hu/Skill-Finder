Fern Health is building pioneering digital care solutions that help people with chronic back or joint pain avoid the need for surgery, keeping them at work and doing the things they love.  We are looking for a data scientist with experience in healthcare to join our team in New York.  Working with our talented team of engineers, product managers, and clinicians, you’ll lead the effort in building a world-class data intelligence platform that unlocks customer insights, informs new product features, marketing initiatives and drives strategic company programs.  If you live and breathe customer engagement, retention and predictive analytics and are well practiced in machine learning techniques spanning but not limited to supervised and unsupervised learning, predictive modeling and recommendation models then we’d love to hear from you.  About the Role  We are looking for a person with experience working with customer and treatment-centric healthcare data. The perfect candidate will be a data modeller, statistician and storyteller, comfortable working with different types of datasets to identify and inform on a broad scope of company initiatives.  This role will give you the opportunity to: Lead our data science practice and capabilities from scratch, driving machine learning implementations using a variety of approaches including NLP, predictive, learning, collaborative filtering, clustering, classification and many more. Report directly to the CTO and work closely with engineers, product managers and clinicians to build mature data intelligence and analytics capabilities. Operate in a highly visible and mission-critical role to identify and drive new product and customer features Work with large clinical research, outcomes and customer engagement datasets to unlock new insights to inform company initiatives. Qualifications we’re looking for: 5-10 years of experience working as a data scientist with the majority earned in healthcare or clinical research. Excellent working knowledge of Jupyter Notebooks or similar tools and strong proficiency in Python, R or both Significant experience working with large datasets in the domains of customer health and clinical data. Strong proficiency with BI tools such as Tableau, Looker etc. Have experience deploying models to production on cloud platforms such as AWS (Sagemakter), GCP or MS Azure Deep understanding and proven experience in Machine Learning techniques including but not limited to statistical and predictive modeling, clustering, classification, recommendation & optimization. Expert level knowledge of SQL Ph.D. or MA qualification in mathematics, statistics, data science or similar Experience working in cross-functional and collaborative teams Healthcare experience is a mustSDL2017
