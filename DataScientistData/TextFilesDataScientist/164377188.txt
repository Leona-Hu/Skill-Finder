Title- Senior Statistical Analyst/Data Science Analyst-PHARMALocation- Stamford, CT (relo provided, Metro North train 4 min walk)Salary - $90-115k + bonus (DOE)Consultant- Eda Zullo, ezullo@smithhanley.comMy client, a management consulting boutique agency, is seeking a full time " Senior Statistical Analyst/Data Science Analyst  " for their Pharma analytics group. This consulting agency (primary clients are in the Pharma/Health Science space) is responsible for illustrative data modeling/statistical analyses. This includes: *linear and logistic regression* market sizing and segmentation analysis* profiling of purchase, usage and demand* customer targeting; predictive models and forecasting* measurement of promotional response.In this role you will be in charge of project modules in client engagements, such as model development, market research, and/or vendor management. You will report to Management Consultants and/or Partners while working on multiple engagements. Job Requirements:*MA Degree from an outstanding academic institution in marketing, statistics, economics, etc. *2-5 years work experience with strong analytic and quantitative background . * Excellent inter-personal skills, able to communicate complex ideas effectively - both verbally and in writing.*Expertise with statistical packages i.e.SPSS, SAS SQL.
