 Summary: The data scientist role is a key interface between the Information Management department and other analytics teams, business unit(s) and various other departments. The data scientist plays a pivotal role in planning, executing and delivering machine learning-based projects. The bulk of this role’s work is in machine learning (ML) modelling, management and problem analysis, data exploration and preparation, and data collection and integration.
