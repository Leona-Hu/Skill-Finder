Work with life sciences organizations from top 40 pharma to breakthrough biotechs and devices companies on their most pressing clinical and commercial challenges. As part of a global consulting firm focused in the pharmaceuticals sector, you have a role to play whether in the development and use of Real World Evidence, big data analytics, pipeline and asset strategy, or products strategy.   The opportunities are for engagement leaders, able to develop enriched client relationships, identifying opportunities to create and add value, while leading consultancy teams of data scientists, business consultants, technologists and clinical experts. Prior management consultancy experience, while certainly useful, is not prerequisite.   What’s on offer is a collaborative, diverse culture and a team ambitious for the potential of life changing therapies and services. Specialist content areas work together throughout the product lifecycle, focusing on innovation to ‘move the needle’.   About You 8+ years experience in any of Real World Evidence, HEOR Research design, Epidemiology Research design, Portfolio and Asset Strategy, Medical Device Marketing Strategy, M&A, Business Development & Licensing, or Patient Registries Design. Advanced qualification e.g. MBA, Ph.D., M.D., M.S., Pharm.D Deep expertise in the life science sector, gained either with a major consultancy firm, a specialist life sciences boutique consultancy or the pharmaceuticals industry in a leadership role working across functions   Location San Francisco, CA. A relocation package is available. Other US locations are open.
