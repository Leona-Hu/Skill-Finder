About the JobPhylagen is seeking a Data Scientist with industry experience and deep expertise in computational analyses of the microbiome. Reporting to the VP of Data Science, you will develop and perform analysis of environmental microbiome datasets for a variety of commercial applications. Working closely with the wet lab and commercial teams, this person will take technical ownership of microbiome analysis pipelines and provide strategic leadership in commercializing the technology.About PhylagenPhylagen, Inc. is a data analytics company leveraging machine learning to harness the vast, unseen world of microbiomes. The microbiome consists of trillions of interacting bacteria, fungi, and pollen collectively present in dust that inhabit every inch of the world. They not only influence our health and well-being, they also provide information about the origin of objects and raw materials.Phylagen is building a data platform by digitizing the DNA of microbiomes found in places and on objects and creating a unique, identifiable fingerprint. This fingerprint is then applied to a variety of solutions in global supply chains for customers ranging from farmers to retail brands to governments.
